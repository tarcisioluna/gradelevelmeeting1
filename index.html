Grade-Level Meeting

Submit your student concerns or grade-wide trends 48 hours before the meeting

Important: Maximum 6 students can be discussed per meeting (first submitted, first discussed). All submissions must include data, not opinions, and require prior conversation with the student.

FAST Protocol

F

What's the specific issue? Be concrete and data-driven.

A

What interventions have you already attempted?

S

What specific help do you need from the team?

T

What did the student say when you talked to them about this concern?

Anything else you would like to share?

GRADE Protocol

G

Briefly describe the group issue that you want to bring to the team.

R

What specific behaviors have you observed that impact class dynamics? Are there specific students who contribute to the problem more than others?

A

Patterns in assessment performance?

D

Any grade-wide social patterns or peer pressure issues?

E

Are there external factors impacting learning?
